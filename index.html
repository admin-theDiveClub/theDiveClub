Whether you're a casual player, league competitor, or tournament regular, The Dive Club is your new home for tracking your journey through cue sports — and soon, much more.

At its core, The Dive Club is a free platform that helps players log their match-play, track performance, and build a personal profile over time. Whether you're competing seriously or just keeping score with friends at your local club, our tools are here to support you.

Why Join?

  • Completely FREE for everyone.
  • Track Every Game: Log your matches, wins, and stats across formats.
  • Live Scorecards & Scoreboards: Let your friends and followers see the action in real-time.
  • Personal Profiles: Showcase your stats and match history with pride.
  • Built-In Timers: Perfect for structured play or casual sessions.

And this is just the beginning. We're already working on features for:

  • Leagues and Tournament Support
  • Multi-sport Expansion
  • Custom Club Integrations

We’re building this together — with players, for players. If you're part of a club or league and want to offer real value to your players, we’d love to hear from you.

Welcome aboard. Let’s make every frame count.
